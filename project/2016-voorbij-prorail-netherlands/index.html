Pile drivers operator

Currently I am working in Delft with the company Voorbij building the foundation for a railway tunnel, operating the KOBELCO BM700-C. This will be a short project of about 3 or 4 weeks.

ProRail takes care of the maintenance and the extensions of the railway infrastructure in The Netherlands. They are preparing the current infrastructure to be able to apply the new ‘Programme Hogefrequentie Spoorvervoer (PHS)’ which means that there will be a train every 10 minutes on the busiest stations.
